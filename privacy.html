Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

INN-OS ("the Bot") is a Discord bot designed to enhance Discord's existing community features.

Information We Collect

The Bot may process Discord-related data required for its functionality, including:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • User-created profiles and preferences
  • Notification and recruitment settings

How Information Is Used

Collected information is used solely to provide and operate the Bot's features, including recruitment systems, profile systems, notification systems, and community management functions.

Data Sharing

INN-OS does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties except where required by law.

Data Removal

Server administrators may remove the Bot from their server at any time. Data associated with removed servers may be deleted during routine maintenance.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the INN-OS support team.
